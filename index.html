About Le Eisvóre

Le Eisvóre is a fragrance brand that embodies elegance with a cool and exclusive aura. Crafted from high-quality ingredients, each variant is designed to last 8-12 hours, ensuring a long-lasting and captivating scent.

We prioritize luxury in simplicity, offering perfumes with strong sillage, premium packaging, and an affordable price (117K/30ml). Le Eisvóre is perfect for those who want to exude class without being excessive.
